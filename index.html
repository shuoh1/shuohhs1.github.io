About Me

I am a postdoctoral scholar at the Istituto Italiano di Tecnologia in Genova, Italy, working with Prof. Lorenzo Rosasco. My research lies at the intersection of statistical learning theory, deep learning theory, approximation theory, and kernel methods. I am particularly interested in generalization analysis of neural networks, operator learning, functional data analysis, and learning under low-dimensional or compositional structure.

I received my PhD in Mathematics from City University of Hong Kong under the supervision of Prof. Han Feng and Prof. Ding-Xuan Zhou. My thesis was titled Mathematical Analysis of Deep Neural Networks.
